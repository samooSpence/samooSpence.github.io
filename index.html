S23 EEC 172 Final Project: Ultimate UART Arcade!

Spencer Moore and Nicolas Sukardy

What it is

A simple game built entirely on the CC3200 Launchpad Board! It synchronizes in real time between each player, giving a true multiplayer experience.

Rules of the Game

Tilt to move the board. The left button will create a temporary shield, that blocks up to 2 orbs of the opponent, but all of yours. You can freely move through shields! The right button launches an orb, which is the key to winning. Getting hit 13 times will end the game!
